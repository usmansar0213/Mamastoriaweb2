Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve your experience, and provide analytics to website operators.

MamaStoria uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to deliver, secure, and improve our AI-powered storytelling platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function. They enable core features such as:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in to your account
  • Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Session Management: Remember your actions during a browsing session
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across servers

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to work properly.

2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with MamaStoria:

  • Usage Analytics: Track pages visited, time spent, and feature usage
  • Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Performance Monitoring: Measure load times and platform speed
  • A/B Testing: Test new features and improvements

Third-party services: We may use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar tools. These services may set their own cookies.

3. Functionality & Personalization Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences:

  • Language Preferences: Remember your selected language
  • Reading History: Track stories you've read for "Continue Reading"
  • Content Recommendations: Personalize story suggestions based on interests
  • Display Settings: Remember theme preferences (dark mode, font size)

4. Advertising & Marketing Cookies (Optional)

If we display ads, these cookies may be used to:

  • Show relevant ads based on your interests
  • Limit how many times you see the same ad
  • Measure ad campaign effectiveness

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser or device settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal
  • AI Services: Cloud AI providers for content generation
  • Social Media: If you share content to social platforms

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies. We do not control third-party cookies.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Ad Personalization: Your Online Choices
  • Do Not Track: Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings

⚠️ Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without impacting core features.

Cookie Duration

Cookies may be:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (days, months, or years) or until manually deleted

The duration varies depending on the cookie type and purpose. Most cookies expire within 1-2 years.
