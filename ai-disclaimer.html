AI Usage Disclaimer

Last updated: January 2026

MamaStoria uses artificial intelligence to assist with creative content creation.

This disclaimer explains how our AI tools work, their limitations, and your responsibilities when using AI-generated content.

1. AI as a Creative Assistant

MamaStoria's AI tools are designed to assist and augment human creativity, not replace it. Our AI features include:

  • Story Generation: Plot ideas, character development, dialogue suggestions
  • Image Creation: Character designs, scene illustrations, comic panels
  • Comic Layout: Panel arrangement and visual sequencing
  • Text-to-Speech: Voice narration for multi-format storytelling

These tools provide suggestions and drafts that require human review, editing, and creative direction to become polished final works.

2. AI Output Limitations & Risks

A. Accuracy & Reliability

AI-generated content may contain:

  • Factual Inaccuracies: AI may generate incorrect information, especially for real-world facts
  • Logical Inconsistencies: Plot holes, character contradictions, or timeline errors
  • Grammatical Errors: Awkward phrasing or incorrect sentence structure
  • Cultural Insensitivity: Unintended stereotypes or inappropriate content

B. Non-Uniqueness

AI systems are trained on vast datasets and may generate similar outputs for different users:

  • AI-generated content is not guaranteed to be unique
  • Similar prompts may produce similar results across users
  • AI cannot verify originality against all existing works
  • Other creators may independently generate similar content

C. Copyright & IP Concerns

AI-generated content may inadvertently resemble existing copyrighted works:

  • AI models are trained on public data that may include copyrighted material
  • Outputs may contain elements similar to existing stories, characters, or art
  • Copyright laws regarding AI-generated content are still evolving
  • Some jurisdictions may not grant copyright protection to pure AI outputs

3. Your Responsibilities as a User

When using AI tools on MamaStoria, you agree to:

Review & Verify

  • Carefully review all AI-generated content before publication
  • Verify factual information and correct inaccuracies
  • Edit for quality, consistency, and appropriateness
  • Ensure content meets platform guidelines and standards

Add Human Creativity

  • Modify and personalize AI outputs with your unique creative vision
  • Provide substantial human authorship and editorial judgment
  • Don't publish raw, unedited AI outputs
  • Combine AI suggestions with your own ideas and storytelling

Ensure Legal Compliance

  • Verify that content doesn't infringe on existing copyrights
  • Conduct reasonable searches for similar existing works
  • Respect intellectual property rights of others
  • Assume full responsibility for published content

Commercial Use Caution

Before using AI-generated content commercially (selling, licensing, merchandising):

  • Consult with legal counsel regarding IP ownership
  • Ensure substantial human modification and creativity
  • Consider copyright registrability in your jurisdiction
  • Understand that pure AI outputs may not be copyrightable

AI technology and related laws are rapidly evolving:

  • AI models are continuously updated and improved
  • Output quality and characteristics may change over time
  • Legal frameworks for AI-generated content are developing
  • Copyright laws may be updated to address AI

We will update this disclaimer to reflect significant changes in technology or legal requirements. Check back regularly for updates.

7. Disclosure & Transparency

We encourage transparency about AI use:

  • Consider disclosing AI assistance in your creator bio or story descriptions
  • Be honest with readers about your creative process
  • Build trust through transparency

While not mandatory, disclosure can help set appropriate expectations and build credibility with your audience.

8. Best Practices for AI-Assisted Creation

Recommended Workflow

  1. Generate: Use AI to create initial drafts or suggestions
  2. Review: Carefully read and evaluate all outputs
  3. Edit: Modify, rewrite, and personalize content
  4. Verify: Check for accuracy, originality, and quality
  5. Iterate: Refine through multiple rounds of editing
  6. Finalize: Ensure substantial human creativity and judgment
  7. Publish: Take ownership of the final work
